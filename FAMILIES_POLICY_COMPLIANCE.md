## üìã Play Console Configuration Required

### Step 1: Target Audience Settings
1. Go to **Play Console** ‚Üí Your App
2. Navigate to **Policy** ‚Üí **App content**
3. Go to **Target audience and content**
4. Select: **"Yes, this app is designed for children"** or **"Yes, this app is designed for families"**
5. Complete the questionnaire:
   - Primary audience: Children/Families
   - Content rating: Everyone (E)
   - Interactive elements: None

### Step 2: Privacy Policy
1. Create a privacy policy stating:
   - App doesn't collect personal information
   - All data stored locally
   - No third-party data sharing
   - No ads, no tracking
2. Host it online (GitHub Pages, your website, etc.)
3. Add the URL in Play Console ‚Üí **Policy** ‚Üí **Privacy policy**

### Step 3: Content Rating
1. Complete the **Content Rating Questionnaire** in Play Console
2. Select appropriate ratings:
   - **ESRB**: Everyone (E)
   - **PEGI**: 3+
   - Answer all questions honestly

### Step 4: App Category
1. Set app category: **Games** ‚Üí **Puzzle** or **Board**
2. Mark as **Family-friendly**
