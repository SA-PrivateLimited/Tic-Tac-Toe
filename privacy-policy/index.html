Tic Tac Toe Game

This privacy policy applies to the Tic Tac Toe app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by SA-Private Limited (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information from users. All game data, including player scores and betting points, is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Local Data Storage

The Application stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game Scores: Win/loss records for Player X and Player O
  • Player Balances: Points accumulated through the betting system
  • Bet Amounts: The last set betting amount

This data is stored using AsyncStorage (Android's local storage mechanism) and remains on your device. It is not accessible to the Service Provider or any third parties.

Third Party Access

The Application does not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are implemented in this Application.

Children's Privacy

The Application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As the app stores data only locally and does not transmit any information, it is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all game data by:

  • Using the "Reset Points" and "Reset Scores" buttons within the Application
  • Clearing the Application's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the Application

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device and the Application does not transmit any information over the internet, your data security is entirely within your control through your device's security measures.
